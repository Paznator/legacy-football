Frequently Asked Questions

When does the beta testing begin?
Beta testing is scheduled to begin on July 1, 2025. All registered beta testers will receive an email with instructions on how to access the beta version.
How many beta testers will be accepted?
We're limiting the beta test to 500 participants to ensure we can properly collect and implement feedback. Sign up early to secure your spot!
Do I need to own cryptocurrency to participate?
For the beta test, no. We'll provide you with test tokens to use during the beta period. However, for the full release, you'll need a MetaMask wallet and some cryptocurrency for transactions.
Will my progress carry over to the full release?
Yes, all progress made during the beta test will carry over to the full release. Your player NFTs, team progress, and any rewards earned will be available in the official game.
How can I provide feedback during the beta?
We'll have a dedicated Discord channel for beta testers where you can report bugs, suggest improvements, and discuss your experience with the development team and other testers.
How long will the beta test last?
The beta test is planned to run for approximately 4 weeks, but we may extend it based on the feedback we receive and any improvements we want to implement.
